Things To Do In The Morning In Downtown Sanford

  • Molly Underberg
  • Trending
  • 05 February 2022

Have you ever ventured down to the beautiful Lake Monroe lakefront in Downtown Sanford on an early Saturday or Sunday morning? If you have, you know there is not a sight like it! The sun glistening over the water, and the calm breeze that brushes over your face. It is a wonderful way to start your morning! But, there is a lot more to enjoy in the downtown area to do after you enjoy a relaxing walk along Lake Monroe and some of these can even include your four-legged friend you brought to the lake with you!

If you are anything like me, you need your little boost of caffeine in the morning. Downtown Sanford has quite a few coffee shops that are within walking distance of where you might have parked your car. Caffe Di Riverwalk, is one of my favorites, it is right along the lake and next to Executive Cigar Shop and Lounge. The size and quality of this cup of joe is wonderful and you can enjoy it outside with your four-legged friend while getting a great view of Lake Monroe. Other coffee shops in the area include Mahogany Coffee in Henry’s Depot and Palate Coffee Brewery.

Saturdays in Downtown Sanford offer another exciting venture to have in the morning, and that is the Sanford Market in Magnolia Square. This farmers market takes place every Saturday from 9am through 3pm. Enjoy #sanfording by scrounging through different vendors offering things such as fresh produce, home baked goods, and homemade unique merchandise. Just like a lot of things in Downtown Sanford, our four-legged friends are invited to join in the fun at the Sanford Market.

Brunch is one of the top things people think about when it comes to a weekend morning. Well if you are in the area, go and enjoy brunch at The Old Jailhouse on Sundays. A reservation will need to be made since this is a pretty popular spot, but their menu offers a lot of good easy choices for brunch; including biscuits and gravy, chicken and waffles, and their jailhouse omelets. Brunch would not be complete without the booze! The Old Jailhouse offers $5 mimosas and $9 bloody mary’s; along with $3.50 domestics and $5 import beers.

Now all through this article I’ve mentioned places that you could bring your four-legged friends on a morning in Downtown Sanford. The best place you could take them that tops all of the places I mentioned is the Paw Park of Historical Sanford! This is a wonderful clean park that I tend to take my own dog to pretty often! Everyone there, human or dog, is friendly and everyone is there to enjoy the space with their animals. This park offers multiple water and poop bag stations as well as some agility course elements if your pet has tons of energy. This is a great place to get some fresh air and let your four-legged friend have some fun in the morning!
