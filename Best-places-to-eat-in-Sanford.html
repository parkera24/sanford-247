Best Places To Eat In Sanford Fl

  • Molly Underberg
  • Trending
  • 8 February 2022

The Historic Downtown Sanford is definitely a unique environment unlike any other. A vibrant and welcoming community encased in solid well-versed red brick streets. Historic Downtown Sanford has everything for a wide range of ages. One thing that anyone can enjoy while visiting, is the phenomenal array of different eateries that are offered along the streets of Historic Sanford. There is anything from restaurants run by award-winning, well-known chefs for those with high quality palates, to the monthly food truck fiesta, with over 20 options that can accommodate many different appetites and ages. Throughout this article we will list five of the best places to eat in Historic Downtown Sanford.

Fuel BBQ

If you are in the mood for some good old school barbeque cooked slow and low. You have to stop by Fuel BBQ located at 104 S Park Ave and experience the best barbeque by the best pitmaster in the area. Fuel was created by the owners of “The Alley”, which is the home to live blues in Central Florida for over 13 years. Fuel offers different types of delicious smoked meats in many different sizes such as mini all the way up to ½ lb. Their range of delectable sides adds a great addition to anything on their menu. Make sure if you go that you try their burnt ends. You can’t leave Sanford without trying Fuel BBQ.

El Zocalo

Have you ever dreamed about going to Mexico and enjoying authentic Mexican cuisine? Look no further than the beautiful streets of Historic Downtown Sanford and go to enjoy El Zocalo Mexican restaurant. Indulge your taste buds in the food that really makes you feel like part of the family. The service is wonderful and the food is amazing! You can never go wrong with their chips and queso as an appetizer to your meal and having an enchilada as your main course! Everything is great when you visit the best Mexican restaurant in Sanford!

The original Christo’s

When you visit Downtown Sanford, you can feel how important family really is to the city. One place that it really shows is at The Original Christo’s on 1st street. The owners have a mission to deliver real food for real people, and it really shows in the classic and essential American dishes that they have on the menu. One thing that makes Christo’s a must stop place on your visit to Sanford is the multitude of fresh desserts that are made fresh every day. They are to die for!

Breezeway Restaurant and Bar

Do you enjoy the beach and the food that goes along with it? Downtown Sanford has our very own beachy cuisine at The Breezeway Restaurant and Bar. Their scratch kitchen combines the freshest and best ingredients to make a fusion of fresh seafood and southern flare that gives an unbelievable twist on surf and turf. Enjoy eating your meal inside or outside in a courtyard and relax in between two historic Sanford buildings while listening to a diverse assortment of local musical artists. The Breezeway is truly unlike anything in Sanford!

Hollerbach’s Willow Tree Cafe

If you only had one night in Downtown Sanford and you asked me what is the one place I have to try before I leave, I would immediately tell you to go to Hollerbach’s Willow Tree Cafe. Not only for the food, but for the experience of it. This is a family run business that has grown with the city of Sanford since opening in 2001 by adding a bar lodge and a rooftop Biergarten in 2020. The food is out of this world! It really makes you feel like you are eating dishes directly from Germany. The environment throughout the restaurant is uplifting and fun! From the time you walk in, you are embraced by traditional German drinking songs that just add to the whole experience you get while at Hollerbach’s. Overall, Hollerbach’s is a must stop place while you visit Historic Downtown Sanford and anyone you ask will tell you the same. It truly is a gem and staple in Historic Downtown Sanford!
